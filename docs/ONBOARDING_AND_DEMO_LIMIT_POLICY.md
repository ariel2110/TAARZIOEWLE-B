
# ONBOARDING AND DEMO LIMIT POLICY

## Demo limit
Customers may request up to 2 demo websites per month in the public onboarding flow.

## Why this exists
- keeps the service focused
- prevents abuse
- supports quality over quantity

## How to identify the customer
The preferred MVP identity key is phone number.

## First access
Customers start with:
- unique temporary password
- forced password change on first login

## Future improvements
- OTP / magic link
- package-aware limits
- customer-level usage dashboard
