# PRIVACY AND VISIBILITY POLICY

## Principle
Use least-privilege visibility.

## Admin
Admins may view all operational and customer data required to run the platform.

## CEO Agent
The CEO Agent may analyze customer-related operational data internally, but must not expose unnecessary private details across unrelated contexts.

## Customer
A customer may only view data tied to their own customer account, business, and site.

## Internal-only data examples
- admin notes
- recommendation rationale
- internal lead quality notes
- risk flags not meant for customers

## Audit
Login attempts, password changes, customer updates, and access-sensitive actions should be logged.
